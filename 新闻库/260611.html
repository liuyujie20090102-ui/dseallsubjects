洪聖廟與赤柱清真寺列為法定古蹟
📰 新聞原文
Stanley Mosque and Stanley Prison and Hung Sing Temple in Wan Chai as statutory monuments. Hung Sing Temple, located on Queen's Road East, is believed to have been built between 1847 and 1852, the Antiquities and Monuments Office says. It has long served as the centre for worship and community activities, while retaining a high degree of its original design. Meanwhile, the Stanley Mosque was built between 1936 and 1937, and is the second oldest mosque in the city. It was built with the effort and donations of Muslim staff working for the Prison Department at the time, as well as the wider community. It is said to stand as a testament to the growth of the Muslim community in Hong Kong.
📝 新聞概要
这则新闻报导了香港两座历史建筑——湾仔洪圣庙和赤柱清真寺——被列为法定古迹。洪圣庙位于皇后大道东，据信建于1847至1852年间，长期作为宗教拜祭和社区活动中心，至今仍高度保留原有设计。赤柱清真寺建于1936至1937年，是香港第二古老的清真寺，由当时的监狱部门穆斯林职员及更广泛的社群捐款兴建，被视为香港穆斯林社群发展的见证。

📐 句式卡
It is believed to have been + 過去分詞 + 時間
公式It is believed to have been + 過去分詞 + 時間
解釋被動語態表示「據信……」，用於引用不確定的來源或歷史推測。to have been 表示對過去已經發生的事的推測。
原文例句It is believed to have been built between 1847 and 1852. 據信它建於1847年至1852年間。
仿寫例句The temple is believed to have been used for community gatherings. 據信這座廟曾用於社區聚會。
適用文體新聞報導、歷史介紹、文化類寫作
It is said to stand as a testament to + 名詞
公式It is said to stand as a testament to + 名詞/名詞短語
解釋表示「據說它是……的見證」，用於描述某物象徵性地證明了某個事實或歷史。
原文例句It is said to stand as a testament to the growth of the Muslim community. 據說它是穆斯林社群發展的見證。
仿寫例句The building stands as a testament to the skills of ancient craftsmen. 這座建築是古代工匠技藝的見證。

while retaining + 名詞
公式主句, + while retaining + 名詞/名詞短語
解釋while 表示「同時」，retaining 是現在分詞，表示「在保留……的同時」，強調在變化中保持原有特徵。
原文例句It has long served as the centre for worship, while retaining a high degree of its original design. 它長期作為敬拜中心，同時保留了高度的原始設計。
仿寫例句The temple has been renovated several times, while retaining its original design. 這座廟經過多次翻新，同時保留了原始設計。
