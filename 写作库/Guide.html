Guide 寫作
農場/莊園 · 開頭段模板
開頭段要素表
要素通用句式範文例句中文翻譯說明
歡迎語 Welcome to ______（名称）! Welcome to Hong Kong Adventure Farm! 欢迎来到香港冒险农场！ 开头第一句，直接打招呼，拉近距离。
地點+年份 Located in ______（地点）, it was established in ______（年份）. Located in Sai Kung, it was established in 2010. 它位于西贡，成立于2010年。 Located in... 过去分词开头，一句讲完地点和年份。
創辦人 Founded by ______（创办人）, a nature lover who wanted to share his passion with others. Founded by Peter Chan, a nature lover who wanted to share his passion with others. 由 Peter Chan 创办，他是一位自然爱好者，想与他人分享他的热情。 Founded by 接人名，a nature lover 补充身份，who wanted to 讲创办原因。
宗旨 It aims to ______（宗旨）. It aims to teach visitors to treasure the environment and reconnect with nature. 它旨在教导游客珍惜环境并与大自然重新连结。 aims to + 动词原形，讲这个农场想做到什么。
亮點預告 Here, you can enjoy activities ranging from ______（活动）to ______（活动）. Here, you can enjoy activities ranging from fruit picking to lakeside camping. 在这里，你可以享受从采摘水果到湖边露营的各种活动。 Here 自然过渡，ranging from...to... 列举活动，为下文铺垫。
完整範文
Welcome to Hong Kong Adventure Farm! Located in Sai Kung, it was established in 2010. Founded by Peter Chan, a nature lover who wanted to share his passion with others, it aims to teach visitors to treasure the environment and reconnect with nature. Here, you can enjoy activities ranging from fruit picking to lakeside camping.
欢迎来到香港冒险农场！它位于西贡，成立于2010年。由 Peter Chan 创办，他是一位自然爱好者，想与他人分享他的热情，它旨在教导游客珍惜环境并与大自然重新连结。在这里，你可以享受从采摘水果到湖边露营的各种活动。
